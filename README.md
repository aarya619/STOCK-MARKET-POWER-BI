# 📖 Project Overview

The **Stock Market Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive **Business Intelligence (BI)** solution developed in **Power BI** using historical Indian stock market data.

This dashboard enables users to monitor stock performance, evaluate market trends, analyze volatility, compare sector-wise performance, and explore trading activity through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

✅ Analyze stock price movements

✅ Monitor daily and monthly market trends

✅ Compare sector-wise stock performance

✅ Evaluate market volatility

✅ Track trading volume patterns

✅ Identify Bullish & Bearish market conditions

✅ Support data-driven investment decisions

# 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 💰 Average Closing Price | Overall market average closing price |
| 📈 Maximum Closing Price | Highest closing price recorded |
| 📉 Minimum Closing Price | Lowest closing price recorded |
| 📦 Total Trading Volume | Total traded stock volume |
| 📏 Average Daily Range | Average daily price fluctuation |

# 💡 Key Insights

🌾 Agriculture sector recorded the highest average volatility.

🚆 Railways sector generated the highest trading volume.

📈 July experienced the highest overall market activity.

🟢 Bullish stocks outnumbered bearish stocks during the analysis period.

🏆 OFSS achieved one of the highest average closing prices.

📊 Sector-wise comparison highlights significant differences in trading volume and price movement.

📅 Interactive slicers enable company-specific and date-wise analysis.
